# Blender Mastery

Welcome to the **Blender Mastery** curriculum. This course is designed to take you from a complete beginner to an advanced 3D artist.

## Curriculum Overview
1.  **Navigation and Mesh Basics**: Learn to move and create.
2.  **Transformations and Proportional Editing**: Precision movement and organic shaping.
3.  **Modifiers and Non-Destructive Workflow**: The power of non-permanent changes.
4.  **Sculpting Basics**: Digital clay modeling.
5.  **UV Unwrapping and Texturing**: Mapping 2D images to 3D surfaces.
6.  **Nodes and Shaders**: Creating complex materials.
7.  **Lighting and Rendering Engines**: Eevee vs. Cycles.
8.  **Basic Rigging and Constraints**: Preparing models for movement.
9.  **Keyframe Animation**: Bringing your scenes to life.
10. **Geometry Nodes Introduction**: Procedural generation and modeling.

## How to use this
Each folder contains a `README.md` with a specific problem set. Complete them in order.
If you get stuck, refer to the `HELP.md` in this directory.
