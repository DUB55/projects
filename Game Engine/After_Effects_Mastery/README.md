# After Effects Mastery

Welcome to the **After Effects Mastery** curriculum. This course focuses on motion graphics, visual effects, and compositing.

## Curriculum Overview
1.  **Composition Basics**: The foundation of every project.
2.  **Keyframes and Interpolation**: Mastering the art of movement.
3.  **Masks and Track Mattes**: Cutting and combining layers.
4.  **Shape Layers and Paths**: Creating graphics from scratch.
5.  **Effects and Presets**: Adding style and functionality.
6.  **Text Animation Engines**: Professional typography in motion.
7.  **3D Layers and Cameras**: Moving beyond the flat plane.
8.  **Expression Basics**: Automating with code.
9.  **Motion Tracking and Stabilization**: Locking graphics to video.
10. **Rendering and Optimization**: Delivering your final masterpiece.

## How to use this
Each folder contains a `README.md` with a specific problem set. Complete them in order.
If you get stuck, refer to the `HELP.md` in this directory.
